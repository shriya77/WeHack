Our Story

RealityCheck was created
to empower real estate investors with actionable insights.

The idea for RealityCheck came from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by investors,
especially those exploring vacation properties. Through research and conversations with industry experts,
we realized how difficult it can be to uncover hidden risks, forecast returns, and make confident investment
decisions. We set out to build a solution that goes beyond basic property listings. RealityCheck combines data
from trusted sources like FEMA, Zillow, and Redfin to provide a comprehensive view of each property. Whether
it's assessing natural disaster risks, analyzing vacation rental potential, or comparing investment options,
RealityCheck is here to simplify real estate investing and help you make smarter decisions.
